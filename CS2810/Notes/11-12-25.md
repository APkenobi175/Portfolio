### Question 1: Which instruction formats are jal and jr

* does jal have a constant?
  * Yes, can't be R-Format
* Does jr need a constant?
  * No
* What does JR actually need
  * 1 Register, Can't be J
* What does jal "address" need?
  * only the address, no registers, so its not R-format
* Final Answer: jal is a J, jr is a R
