1. Wire added from the instruction bits [25-0] and it goes through a left shifter
2. After the data is shifted it goes to another mux to select between BEQ and J
3. That second mux is controlled by a new Jump control
4. Logic for the contoller, update the controller to turn off everything else so it works.
